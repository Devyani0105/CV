Barbie

Barbie is a Fation Doll manufactured by the American toy company Nattel, Tnc. and launched in March 1959. American Businesswomen Ruth Handler is credited with the creation of the doll using a German doll called Bied Lilli as her inspiration. More Details

What is so special about Barbie?

  • Mattel created the first doll in a wheelchair.
  • In 1997, the company debuted share a Smile Becky.
  • Barbie was an "It" girl from day one.
  • Barbie was originally marketed as a "Teenage Fashion Model".
  • Around 350,000 dolls were sold during the first year.
